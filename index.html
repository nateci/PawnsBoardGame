üìò Full Game Instructions

PawnsBoard is a two-player strategic board game where players use cards to influence a grid, capture pawns, and score control of rows. Inspired by Queen‚Äôs Blood from Final Fantasy XVI, each player builds influence using cards with unique 5x5 patterns.

Features:

  • Graphical interface for human vs. human or human vs. AI play
  • Multiple AI strategies: FillFirst, MaximizeRowScore, Minimax, etc.
  • Visualized influence, card selection, and status indicators
  • Two-player turn-based system with scoring

To win: control more rows by scoring higher than your opponent in each row.
